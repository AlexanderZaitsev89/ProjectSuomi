This amazing app would challenge your knowledge about Finland! If you are smart enough then dare to push the image below ! Good luck!

Description

When you run the application, you would see a map of Finland.Above the map there would be a question that you should answer and also a number of right answers will be displayed there. All the questions are connected with the geographical location of the towns.In order to answer- click on the mmap where you think town is situated.After you click a red dot would appear-this is location that you clicked and a black area-this is the actual location.When you answer a question click NEXT button. Wish you luck!
